About Us

We are one of the best event planners in Texas. ADM is an event Management Company specializing in creating, planning, and executing weddings, B2B and B2C corporate marketing events, school and college reunions, getaways, birthdays and other social functions. Our strong advertising and entertainment techniques help people take action and influence others by coming up with a brand strategy through the amalgamation of the hard work of your team with ours.

Our expertise includes events of all types, sizes, and budgets. ADM Event Management handles events according to the needs and goals of an individual client.
